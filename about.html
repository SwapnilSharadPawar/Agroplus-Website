Get to know about our company

Agroplus is a growing agritech and software solutions company based in Pargaon Tarfe Ale, Taluka Junnar, District Pune. We are dedicated to empowering farmers and agri-businesses with modern technology, smart farm management tools, and reliable consulting services. Our mission is to combine traditional farming knowledge with innovative digital solutions to support sustainable agriculture and business growth.

At Agroplus, we provide services such as farm analysis & consulting, accounting and management solutions, market analysis & statistics, and digital record-keeping tools. Our goal is to empower farmers with reliable data, smart tools, and expert guidance that simplify decision-making and ensure sustainable development. We are committed to building a strong bridge between traditional farming practices and modern innovations, ensuring that agriculture not only thrives but also becomes future-ready.
